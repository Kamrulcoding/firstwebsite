Let's learn about British Council. The British Council is a British organisation specialising in international cultural and educational opportunities. It works in over 100 countries: promoting a wider knowledge of the United Kingdom and the English language (and the Welsh language in Argentina[3]); encouraging cultural, scientific, technological and educational co-operation with the United Kingdom.
